# 🛒 Shopping List

A modern, intuitive, and mobile-friendly web application designed to eliminate the frustrations of traditional shopping lists. No more messy paper lists or constantly editing messages on WhatsApp/Telegram while walking through store aisles.

🔗 \*\*Live Demo: https://shopping-list-xi-umber.vercel.app/
---

## 💡 Why I Built This App

A long time ago, I used to write my shopping lists on a piece of paper. But once I was at the store, it was a hassle to always carry a pen to cross things off. Plus, items on the shelves are never in the same order as a written list. Sometimes, I even had to visit multiple stores to finish my shopping, which made the paper list even messier.

Later, I tried messaging the list to myself on apps like WhatsApp or Telegram. However, while shopping, I constantly had to keep the message in "edit mode" just to update it, which wasn't convenient at all.

Driven by these everyday frustrations, I decided to build this app. I wanted to create a simple, smart, and hassle-free tool that redefines the shopping experience, making it organized, seamless, and fully responsive on any device.

---

## 🚀 Features

- **Quick Add & Edit:** Easily add items to your list on the go.
- **One-Tap Check-off:** Cross items off your list instantly with a simple tap.
- **Persistent Storage:** Your list stays safe even if you refresh the browser (via LocalStorage/Database).
- **Responsive Design:** Optimized perfectly for mobile screens so you can use it easily with one hand while shopping.
- **Clean UI:** Built with professional and accessible components for a smooth user experience.
